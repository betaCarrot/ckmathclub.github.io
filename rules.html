Rules

Math Club meets every Wednesday C/W period in Math Office.

If you would like to join math club and appear on the standings, please email chinde@cranbrook.edu or kkatz@cranbrook.edu

Abbreviations

US: USA Mathematical Talent Search

MM: Math Madness

TRT: Treksit Graph Theory Game

MMPC: Michigan Math Prize Competition

Scoring

USAMTS: 20 points for each correct solution emailed to chinde@cranbrook.edu. 5-point bonus for first solution to each problem. 10-point bonus for elegant solution.

Treksit: 50 points for level 20 screenshot emailed to chinde@cranbrook.edu. 20 points for level 10 screenshot. 10-point bonus for first submission.

MMPC (tentative): 5 points for each correct answer

Ranking

To allow absences, two lowest scores on Wednesday math club contests will be dropped when determining ranking.

Absolute score = total score over all contests

Relative score = total score - two lowest scores on Wednesday contests

When two contestants have the same relative score, their absolute scores will be used as tiebreaker.
